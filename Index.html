Meet our professionals

Dr. R K Anand

BV-SC. And AH, C.M.I, S.V.S

Founder/CEO/Senior Veterinary Consultant

DR. R.K. ANAND is working for the welfare and treatment of pets for the last 42 years. Dr. Anand did his Graduation in veterinary Medicine in the year 1967 and Joined A Germen Multinational Veterinary Pharmaceutical Company and successfully completed 15 years in a Managerial Position.

After that in year 1983, he started pets practice from small pets clinic JANAK PURI As there was no qualified vet to give relief to the needy sick pets in whole of West Delhi.

  • Dr RK Anand was awarded the Best Clinic Award in the country by Indian Society for Advancement of Canine Practice in the year 2008 at Chennai by Chief Secretary of Tamil Nadu.
  • Lifetime Achievement Award was presented to Dr RK Anand in 2017 on World Veterinary Day by Canine welfare club of India at New Delhi.
  • ⁠Professional Efficiency Award was awarded to Dr RK Anand by Vetopia at their Annual national conference at Jaipur in 2020.
Since then DR. ANAND has been getting popular day by day and modernizing in equipments and services as per the growing need of the pets. For the last 10 years DR. ANANDS PETS HOSPITAL has grown among the 2 to 3 top pets clinic in DELHI & NCR.

Dr. Gautam Anand

B.V.Sc. & A.H., M.V.Sc. Surgery

Senior Veterinary Consultant

DR. GAUTAM ANAND from School days was keen to join his father practice as he had love liking for pets. So after completing his master in veterinary surgery and radiology join in November 2001. He joined his fathers established DR. ANANDS PETS HOSPITAL.

To further increase is knowledge and technique in treatment of pets in advanced countries like U.S.A./EUROPE, he under gone following trainings from world known experts.

  • Trained in Ultra Sonography by DR. CLIFORD BERRY NAVC FLORIDA 2011. Trained in advanced Soft Tissue Surgery procedures by DR. HOWARD SIEM NAVC FLORIDA 2017.
  • Cardiology Masters course from ESAVS, LUX EMBOURG, ITALY, SPAIN under DR. GEIRHARD WEISS.
  • Faculty in Cardiology under DR. ADRIAN BOSWOOD AT ROYAL VETRENIARY COLLEGE LONDON.
  • Advanced training in GASTROENTROLOGY AND ENDOSCOPY with DR. TOD TAMS, LASVEGAS.
With above qualification and trainings DR. GAUTAM ANAND fully justified his knowledge in giving relief to be needy sick pets.
He is an asset of DR. ANANDS PETS HOSPITAL.
